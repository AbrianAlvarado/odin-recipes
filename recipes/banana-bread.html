Banana bread

Banana bread is bread primarily made of bananas. Crazy, right? You could say it's... bananas!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Bananas

Preparation

  1. Measure out each of the ingredients
  2. Pour the dry ingredients together
  3. Mix the wet ingredients
  4. Mix the dry and wet together
